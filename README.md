# Red Bull India: Women Audience Data Analysis 🎯

## 📌 Project Overview
An independent data analysis project tracking the market perception, brand awareness gaps, and unique consumption barriers among **500 urban Indian women** across 10 major metropolitan cities. 

This repository translates raw consumer survey data into a business intelligence dashboard with 3 distinct market-expansion recommendations to bridge the gender demographic gap for Red Bull India.

---

## 📊 Key Findings
* ** Product Alienation:** 67 surveyed women explicitly feel the product is "Not for Women" due to current extreme sports marketing profiles.
* ** Health Concerns:** 78 respondents cited dietary, sugar content, and artificial ingredients as their #1 adoption barrier.
* ** Regional Dropoff:** Brand awareness tracking hits its lowest recorded baseline in Kolkata, identifying a critical geographical field marketing gap.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Microsoft Excel:** Raw data cleaning, variable processing, and structuring.
* **Pivot Tables & Charts:** Dynamic cross-examination of city-wise awareness against perception metrics.
* **Canva / PowerPoint:** 5-Slide Executive Pitch Deck development for brand stakeholders.

---

* `/data` - Cleaned and anonymized 500-respondent survey datasets.
